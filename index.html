The Adventure

What is Winds Awaken?

An ocean-spanning adventure inspired by the timeless charm of The Wind Waker and the cozy spirit of A Short Hike.

Set sail across a vast ocean dotted with mystical floating islands. Each island is a handcrafted platforming challenge filled with collectibles, hidden paths, and breathtaking scenery. Master the winds, unlock the Phase Shift ability to transform into a wind spirit, and discover the secrets that bind these islands together.

With 60 levels spread across 6 unique biomes — from sun-kissed tropical beaches to the fiery heart of volcanic mountains — every island tells a story.

Gameplay

Core Features

🏔️

Precision Platforming

Double jumps, wall slides, wall jumps, dashes, and ground pounds. A movement system that feels crisp and satisfying.

🌍

6 Unique Biomes

Tropical beaches, dense jungles, rocky cliffs, charming villages, open ocean, and fiery volcanic islands — each with its own style.

🗺️

60 Handcrafted Levels

Every level is designed with increasing complexity and beauty. The further you go, the more elaborate the world becomes.

💰

Coins & Jade

Collect golden coins and rare jade to unlock secrets. Chase three-star ratings on every level for completionists.

🎨

Cel-Shaded Beauty

Custom toon shaders, stylized water, aurora skies, and lava effects bring this world to life with a gorgeous art style.

🌬️

Phase Shift

Transform into a wind spirit to phase through walls, discover hidden paths, and see the world from a whole new perspective.
