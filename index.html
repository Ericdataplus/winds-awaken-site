About the Game

Sail Across a
Mystical Ocean

Winds Awaken is a cel-shaded 3D platformer inspired by The Wind Waker and A Short Hike. Master the winds, unlock the Phase Shift ability to transform into a wind spirit, and discover the secrets that bind the floating islands together.

Core Mechanics

Built for Adventure

🏔️

Precision Platforming

Double jumps, wall slides, wall jumps, dashes, and ground pounds — every move feels tight and responsive.

🌬️

Phase Shift

Transform into a wind spirit. Pass through walls, reveal hidden paths, and see a secret layer of the world.

🎨

Cel-Shaded Beauty

Custom toon shaders, stylized water, aurora skies, and lava effects — every biome is a visual treat.

💎

Collectibles & Secrets

Golden coins, jade collectibles, three-star ratings, and hidden Easter eggs for the completionists.
